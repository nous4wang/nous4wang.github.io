Dr. Fu Wang is a Senior Engineer at the **CMA Earth System Modeling and Prediction Centre (CEMC)**, China Meteorological Administration, working in the Satellite Data Assimilation Division. He received his Ph.D. in Atmospheric Remote Sensing from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2015.

His research focuses on **satellite data assimilation**, **deep learning for remote sensing**, and their applications to **numerical weather prediction (NWP)**. He has published 50+ papers in peer-reviewed journals and holds 10+ patents. He serves as a **WMO Expert** and was a Visiting Scholar at the University of Macau in 2024, working with Professor Yicong Zhou on machine learning for remote sensing and NWP.
